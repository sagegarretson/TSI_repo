About Techni-Serve, Inc.

Techni-Serve, Inc. was started by two Canon certified service technicians in 1987. The company originally provided fully re-built copiers and fax machines. Because of the business's tremendous growth, they were offered a full line Canon authorized dealership. We can provide new desktop systems all the way up to high end production systems, full color copiers & printers, fax machines, high speed scanners, large format printers, basically everything a business needs to be successful. For businesses that are on a tight budget, we still provide fully rebuilt copiers at huge discounts and guarantee them as if they were new.

Techni-Serve is a locally owned full line Canon copier, fax, color printer authorized dealer. In business for over 30 years, we have grown our company to over 2,500 electronic systems that we currently service. We strive in giving our customers the best products in the market and back that up with the best local service available. We are also a HP Certified Printer repair center. We currently provide service to over 1,000 printers.

Techni-Serve is a certified Canon Alliance Partner
